About Chillana!

Chillana - Shout your tweet.

Chillana is a shout-out platform where people can shout a tweet and "we put it in the map from where and what they shouted". You can see the shouts around you or anywhere in the world. It's basically a Geo-location based, non-persistent twitter kinda platform. Hope it helps.
